New in v18
“Stops” on the left, “steps” up top

Renamed “stages” to stops (fits the journey metaphor, less jargon). Left sidebar lists all 7 stops. Click any unlocked stop, and the top strip reveals its steps — a variable list per stop (Explore has 2 steps, First chat has 3, FDD Playbook has 3, etc.). Each step can be its own content type: slides, application, static info, scheduling widget, video, document, checklist.

Click around to test
Every stop\u2019s steps are wired up

Click the “Brand tour” step in Explore \u2014 slides render inline. Click “Light application” \u2014 launch card to the app. Click any other stop to see its steps as placeholder blocks explaining what content type will live there. Each placeholder shows its type so the team can decide: for Stage 3’s webinar, is that a scheduling widget + video + Q&A page? Configurable per-stop, per-step.

Design notes

  • Chapter is now: Title → Pillars → Video → Quote → Pillars → CTA. Stats moved out of the slide flow and into the persistent info strip, which trades a whole slide for always-on context.
  • Video dot in the progress bar has a small inner circle so candidates can see “oh that one’s a video” before they land on it.
  • Static info strip uses four cells, but can collapse to three or grow to five. The fourth cell uses the brand color as an accent to draw a little attention — good for a differentiator.
  • Video thumbnail is a brand-tinted radial for prototype purposes. In prod it’s the poster frame pulled from the uploaded video.
  • Click the play button to simulate playback. Real embed goes there.
  • Captions stay as the “Blue Maven voice” line below the slide — unchanged from v1.
